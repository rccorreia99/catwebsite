Cats - and How cute they are

The website that shares your love for the little felines

The cat (Felis catus), also referred to as domestic cat or house cat, is a small domesticated carnivorous mammal. It is the only domesticated species of the family Felidae. Here at Cats , we share our love for cats by sharing all the facts and even photos.

Cats have sharp claws and excellent night vision. They love to climb, explore, and cuddle with their humans. Aren't they adorable?
